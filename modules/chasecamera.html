Functions

new (width, height, stiffness, damping, mass)
Creates a new chasecamera.

Parameters:

  • width number Width of the camera's viewport.
  • height number Height of the camera viewport.
  • stiffness number How stiff the camera feels.
  • damping number How fast the camera should lose velocity. The value should be in the range of [0..1]. A value of 1 means the camera will not lose velocity, and a value of 0 means that velocity will not affect the camera.
  • mass number How weighty the camera feels.

Returns:

    chasecamera

chasecamera:clamp (x, y, r, b)
Clamps the camera's viewport within a rectangle. The camera's viewport will not be allowed outside of this rectangle.

Parameters:

  • x number X-coordinate of the rectangle.
  • y number Y-coordinate of the rectangle.
  • r number X-coordinate of the right side of the rectangle.
  • b number Y-coordinate of the bottom side of the rectangle.
